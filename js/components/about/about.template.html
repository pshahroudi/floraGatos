About FloraGatos

Our Story

At FloraGatos, we believe nature shouldn’t be a luxury—it should be part of daily life. With today’s fast-paced lifestyle, many people lose their connection to the natural world. That’s why we created FloraGatos: to make it easy to enjoy the beauty and benefits of indoor plants.

What We Offer

  • Indoor Plants

    From beginner-friendly succulents to bold, leafy houseplants like Monstera and pothos.

  • Terrariums

    Custom-built, glass-jar ecosystems that thrive in any office or home environment. They’re low-maintenance, long-lasting, and designed to bring calm to your space.

  • Accessories

    Decorative pots, soil mixes, and moss poles to support healthy plant growth.

Delivery & Shipping

  • Bay Area Delivery

    Our handcrafted terrariums are too delicate for shipping, so we personally deliver them across the Bay Area at a low cost.

  • Etsy Shop

    For succulents, small plants, and accessories, we ship nationwide via our Etsy store.

Why Choose FloraGatos?

  • Local Los Gatos roots, serving the Bay Area community.
  • A passion for plants and sustainable living.
  • Personal service—whether you’re just starting your plant journey or adding to your collection, we’re here to help.
