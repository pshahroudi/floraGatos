Terrariums

Terrariums are miniature ecosystems enclosed in glass, offering a captivating way to showcase plants while requiring minimal care. Their self-sustaining nature makes them perfect for desks, shelves, or any small space craving a touch of greenery.

  • LECA Balls – Expanded clay pebbles that provide drainage.
  • Mesh – Prevents soil from mixing with the drainage layer.
  • Charcoal – Filters impurities and keeps the terrarium fresh.
  • Substrate – A mix of orchid bark, worm castings, horticultural charcoal, and sphagnum moss, creating a rich growing medium.
  • Plants – The final layer where your chosen greenery thrives.
